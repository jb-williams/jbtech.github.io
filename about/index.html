About

This site is a place for my to try to gather my notes into a coherent posts.

  In Nov. 2020, I completely switched to Linux, doing a step by step command line installation, having never actually used any Unix-like system before, making myself learn if I wanted to use my computer. Going down this path of learning all I could of computers and operating systems drastically increased my interest in the Cyber Security field. This special interest made me realize that I wanted to enter this field and change my career path. I enrolled in a cyber security bootcamp to weave together the knowledge I already had, as well as, expand it and all the other aspects of the field. I completed The University of Kansas Cyber Security Bootcamp in March 2022, and want to break into the cyber security/system admin/IT fields, with my major interests in Penetration Testing. I have become most proficient with Linux systems, but I am constantly attempting to grow my knowledge of computer/network systems and devices. My free time is mostly spent in Vim and Bash, scripting out things I do everyday and tinkering with customizing my minimal Linux GUI laptop setup, as well as playing D&D with old highschool friends. I am currently studying for the Security+, PenTest+, and Linux+ certifications.

  In my previous employment I gained experience with the installation, maintenance, diagnosis, and repair of both commercial and residential HVAC; Ductwork and sealed systems, including all home appliances; Metal Fabrication/Welding; Circuit board diagnosis and repair; Training technicians to work on refrigerators and sealed systems, and provided exceptional customer service and ensured efficient store operation in Food Services Industry.
